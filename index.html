What We Do

Every piece of gear that comes through the shop gets the full treatment — careful diagnosis, honest assessment, and skilled hands.

Repairs

Amplifiers, stereos, guitar pedals, and consumer electronics. Whether it's a blown fuse or a failed output transformer, we find the fault and fix it properly.

Modifications

Tone upgrades, circuit mods, bias adjustments, and custom wiring. Unlock the full potential of your gear with thoughtful, reversible modifications.

Restorations

Vintage gear brought back to factory spec — or better. From classic hi-fi receivers to antique tube amplifiers, we respect the original design while making it reliable.

Custom Builds

From-scratch amp and pedal builds to your exact specification. If you can describe the tone in your head, we can design and build the circuit that gets you there.

About

Aman Audio was founded on a simple conviction: good gear deserves good care. With decades of hands-on experience across tube amplifiers, solid-state circuits, vintage hi-fi, and guitar electronics, we bring a depth of knowledge that goes beyond surface-level repair.

Every job — whether it's a straightforward capacitor replacement or a complete ground-up custom build — gets the same level of attention. We take the time to understand what you're after, explain what we find, and never recommend work that isn't necessary.

This is a one-person shop. When you bring your gear to Aman Audio, you're dealing directly with the person doing the work — no handoffs, no surprises.
